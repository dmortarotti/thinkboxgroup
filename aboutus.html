A Partner Not a Vendor

ThinkBox has an experienced in-house team and a dedicated eco-system with the talent to develop profitable projects, a committment to the environment, and a passion to support under-served communities.

ThinkBox Group, LLC. develops, implements and finances projects in the renewable energy industry including Solar, Biomass, Energy Efficiency and Water. Since 1995 we have developed more than $200M in projects. Our activities have supported our end-use customers in implementing ground-breaking projects that have saved over $30M in energy and operations costs.

We have worked with leading companies, federal agencies, and schools. We are a "roll up the sleeves" organization that develops projects from the initial planning stages through measurement and verification of results. Our experience allows us to work on a diverse range of projects. We have a proven track record over a long time horizon. We look forward to learning about your renewable energy objectives and challenges.
